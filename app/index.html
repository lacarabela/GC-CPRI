About the Index

Composite Scoring

The GC-CPRI combines five access-related indicators into a single composite score using Principal Component Analysis (PCA). PCA identifies underlying variance patterns across indicators and reduces dimensionality while preserving meaningful structure.

Data Sources

Analysis draws on Alabama Secretary of State 2024 general election data and USDA Economic Research Service indicators including poverty estimates, educational attainment, and population data at the county level.

Interpretation

Scores represent relative access-related risk across Alabama counties, not absolute measures of civic engagement or voting behavior. Higher scores indicate a greater concentration of structural access barriers relative to other counties.

Limitations

This tool does not establish causal relationships between indicators and participation outcomes. Coverage currently reflects publicly available data, which may vary by county. All assumptions and data decisions are documented in the project repository.
